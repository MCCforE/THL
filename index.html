About Me

Welcome to the Huang Research Group at the University of North Dakota. Our research focuses on catalysis, electrocatalysis, and mechanistic understanding of chemical transformations relevant to energy and the environment.

We develop well-defined catalytic materials and combine synthesis, kinetic studies, spectroscopy, and theory to understand how active-site structure governs reactivity, selectivity, and stability.

Current interests include heterogeneous catalysis, multi-site catalyst design, carbon dioxide conversion, oxidative dehydrogenation, and operando characterization of catalytic systems.
