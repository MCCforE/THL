WELCOME TO OUR RESEARCH LAB PAGE!

We aim to develop new synthetic strategies for well-defined nanomateirals with atomically precise sizes or crystallographic surface structures that can lead to a fundamental understanding of their structure-property relationship. This new knowledge will be used to design optimized nanomaterials for responding to the current energy crisis and environmental problems.

Current interests include multi-site catalyst design, CO2 reduction, propane dehydrogenation, and operando characterization of catalytic systems.

Let's work together to achieve long-term sustainability!
